Palmera Eco Resorts by King's Hotel

Set against the backdrop of its illustrious history and cultural heritage, this resort is located near the Koneswaram Temple and the historic Trincomalee Fort. Moreover, this pristine resort is a gateway to some of the country's most stunning beaches like Nilaveli and Uppuveli. The resort offers a number of nature trails that lead to whale watching experiences and opportunities to explore underwater wonders through snorkeling expedition

Kingdom Gate Hotel by King's Hotel

The impressive Kingdom Gate Hotel is located in a beautiful forest setting.. Adjacent to the Kandalam Reservoir and close to many local attractions including Sigiriya, a UNESCO World Heritage Site, this hotel is one of the premier hotel in Dambulla. The resort is 3.5 hours (86 miles / 140km) drive from Bandaranaike International Airport.
